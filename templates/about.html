About Us

Waste to Value is dedicated to transforming waste into sustainable resources. Our mission is to empower individuals and businesses to reduce environmental impact through innovative waste classification, recycling solutions, and an eco-friendly marketplace.

Using advanced AI technology, we help you identify recyclable, compostable, and general waste, connecting you with nearby recycling centers. Join us in building a greener future!
